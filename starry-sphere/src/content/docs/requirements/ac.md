## Extensibility

**What does it mean?**
<https://quality.arc42.org/qualities/extensibility>

**Why is it important?**

- The Zamundan chocolate sector is growing dynamically year after year, and many of the market players have unique systems that require unique integration. It is crucial that these integrations can be carried out smoothly.

## Interoperability

**What does it mean?**
<https://quality.arc42.org/qualities/interoperability>

**Why is it important?**
The most important service of the system is that it combines the fragmented catalogs of Zamunda chocolate producers into a single, unified catalog. However, this requires proper cooperation with a wide variety of supplier systems.

## Scalability

**What does it mean?**
<https://quality.arc42.org/qualities/scalability>

**Why is it important?**

Chocolate stocks and prices change very quickly, so frequent updates are to be expected.

- The Zamundan chocolate sector is growing dynamically year after year, which means new market players and increasing workloads.

## Latency

**What does it mean?**

- <https://quality.arc42.org/qualities/latency>

**Why is it important?**

- Chocolate inventories and prices change very quickly, and a consolidated catalog is meaningless if it does not keep up with these changes.
- We want to provide a pleasant user experience for global visitors when browsing.
